Lemonade

Lemonade, certainly a classic and refreshing drink , enjoy this drink with the egg sandwich maybe?

Thirsty?

Ingredients

  • 2 Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Water

Recipe Instructions

  1. Fill your cup of water
  2. Squeeze the lemon juice into said cup
  3. Add sugar to your cup
  4. Stir thorougly
  5. Enjoy
