Egg Sandwich

The Egg sandwich is so peak its nutrituos and can be healthy depending on the ingredients that are used.

Yum

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Mayo

Recipe Instructions

  1. Cook your eggs with oil and some salt
  2. Put some Mayo on your slices of bread
  3. Put the Eggs on the bread
  4. Enjoy
